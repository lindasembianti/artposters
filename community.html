Our Mission

Plakat's main aim is to support and bolster local artists, from Copenhagen, as well as their art, by being a platform where unique and diverse art gets more accessible to admirers and allies. In this way, we hope to positively bring value and a sense of affection to both artists and art lovers.     • Our Values

    Originality

    Every poster has been created with passion and great devotion by a unique artist. We hope to show how diverse and varied can one local community be, while still having a similar bond and sentiment over art and design.

    Support

    We aspire to support and be a platform for local artists from Copenhagen, Denmark by helping them showing their work and passions and inspire visitors to be more creative and have a deeper sense of belonging, in these isolating times.

    Community

    There are so many positive aspects to being part of a group or community. Providing unique opportunities for people to learn from each other and give support and encouragement are an invaluable part of joining forces with our peers.
    However, in recent times, this has been put into rupture. It can feel easy to feel alone and inspired, since we don't connect with our peers as much. Therefore, we aim to build a creative and dynamic community by sharing and organising creative events that can bring artists and interested people together.
